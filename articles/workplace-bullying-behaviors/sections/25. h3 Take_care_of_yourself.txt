
Workplace bullying can be a difficult experience to endure. It can leave you feeling isolated, unsupported, and powerless. It is important to remember that you are not alone and that there are ways to take care of yourself during this difficult time. Taking care of yourself is essential for maintaining your mental health and wellbeing. This can include taking regular breaks to step away from the situation, talking to a trusted friend or family member, or even seeking out professional help. It is also important to remember that workplace bullying is never your fault, and that you have the right to feel safe and supported at work. Taking care of yourself during this time is essential for helping to manage the difficult feelings that can come with workplace bullying behaviors.
