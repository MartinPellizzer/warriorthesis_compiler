
Workplace bullying can have a devastating effect on mental health. It can leave employees feeling anxious, stressed, and isolated, and can even lead to depression. Bullying can take many forms, from direct verbal abuse to subtle exclusion and sabotage. It can be difficult to recognize and even harder to challenge. Victims of workplace bullying often feel powerless and unable to speak up or seek help. This can lead to feelings of helplessness and a sense of being trapped. It is important to recognize the signs of workplace bullying and to take action to protect yourself and your mental health. If you or someone you know is being bullied, reach out for help and support. There are resources available to help you cope with the emotional and psychological effects of workplace bullying.
