
Working in a toxic environment can be difficult and overwhelming, and it can be hard to know who to turn to for help. If you are facing workplace bullying behaviors, it is important to remember that you are not alone and that there are resources available to help. Seeking out help from a trusted friend, family member, or colleague can help you gain the support you need to cope with the situation. Additionally, speaking with a mental health professional can provide you with strategies to manage the situation and help you move forward. Remember, you are not responsible for the bullying behaviors and you have the right to feel safe and secure in your workplace. Do not hesitate to reach out for help if you need it.
