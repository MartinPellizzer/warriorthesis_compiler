
Workplace bullying is a form of harassment that can have a severe impact on the mental and physical health of employees. It includes behaviors such as teasing, belittling, intimidating, and threatening. Bullying can be subtle or overt, and it can be directed at an individual or a group. It can occur in any workplace, from a small business to a large corporation, and can take many forms. It is important to recognize that workplace bullying is a form of abuse and should not be tolerated. Employees should feel safe and supported in their workplace, and employers should take steps to ensure that any bullying behavior is addressed and stopped. If you are experiencing workplace bullying, it is important to reach out for help and support. There are resources available to help you cope with the situation and to report the behavior to your employer.
