
Workplace bullying is an unfortunately all too common experience for many employees. Bullying can take many forms and can have a devastating impact on the mental and physical health of the victims. It is important to be aware of the different types of workplace bullying behaviors so that we can take steps to protect ourselves and our colleagues. Common workplace bullying behaviors include verbal abuse, intimidation, ostracism, manipulation, and humiliation. It is important to remember that bullying is never acceptable and that it is important to speak up and take action if you or someone you know is being bullied. It is also important to remember that there is always help available, and that it is possible to overcome the effects of bullying and create a safe and supportive workplace.
