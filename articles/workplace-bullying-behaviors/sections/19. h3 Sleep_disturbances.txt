
Sleep disturbances can be a difficult and disorienting experience to go through, especially when they are caused by workplace bullying behaviors. It can be hard to know how to cope with the effects of workplace bullying, and it can be even harder to manage the resulting sleep disturbances. It's important to remember that you are not alone in this experience. There are many resources and support groups available to help you through this difficult time. Try to create a calming environment for yourself before bedtime, such as reading a book, meditating, or listening to soothing music. If you find yourself struggling to sleep, talk to a professional who can help you find the best methods for managing your sleep disturbances. With the right support, you can find the strength to get through this challenging time.
