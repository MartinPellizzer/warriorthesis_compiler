
Workplace bullying is an unfortunately common occurrence that can have a devastating effect on those who are subjected to it. It can come in many forms, from persistent criticism and belittling to outright threats and abuse. No one should have to endure such treatment, and it's important to remember that it is never okay. It's important to document any instances of workplace bullying, so that you have a record of what has happened and can take appropriate action if needed. Documenting bullying can also help you to recognize any patterns of behavior that may be occurring, and to make sure that you are not alone in your experience. If you feel like you are being bullied, it's important to reach out for help and support. There are many resources available to those who are being bullied, and it's important to remember that you are not alone.
