
Speak up. It's a phrase that can be intimidating to some, but it's an important skill to have when it comes to addressing workplace bullying behaviors. Bullying can take many forms, from verbal put-downs to exclusion and sabotage. No matter the form, it's important to take a stand against it and speak up. It can be difficult to confront a bully, but it's important to remember that you have the power to make a difference in your workplace. You can help create a safe, supportive environment for yourself and your colleagues by standing up for yourself and others. It's not always easy, but it's worth it. Speaking up can help bring about change and make your workplace a better place for everyone.
